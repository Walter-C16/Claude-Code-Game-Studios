## Long Description (main page body — supports markdown)

```markdown
## Olympus has fallen.

The gods you remember from the myths are gone — or worse, they're alive and
they don't remember who they were. The pantheon shattered, the temples are
dust, and divine power is something you buy in pieces at dirty village
taverns.

You wake up in a crashed craft in a forest that shouldn't exist. The first
face you see is **Artemis**, the huntress goddess, and she has no idea she
used to rule the moon.

**Dark Olympus** is a narrative RPG where every fight is a poker hand, every
dialogue choice is a small betrayal, and every goddess you meet is a broken
god looking for a reason to keep going.

---

## What's in this 0.0.1 demo

- **~30–45 minutes of story**, Chapter 1 from the shattered forest to the
  Gaia Spirit boss fight.
- **One complete romance arc** with Artemis through stage 2 — learn her
  likes, give her the right gifts, watch her trust you.
- **3 major characters fully animated** (Artemis, Hippolyta, the Priestess
  of Gaia) plus five NPCs with portrait sets.
- **Poker-combat core loop**: select cards, score hands, deal damage. Hand
  rank becomes attack power; element reactions turn good hands into great
  hands.
- **Divine Blessings** — passive perks earned through romance, not grinding.
- **Four-round tavern tournament** (optional) for gold grinders.
- **Portrait mobile viewport** (430×932) — designed one-handed for phones,
  plays fine in browser too.

## What's coming in 0.0.2+

- The other 7 companions (Daphne, Circe, Thetis, Echo, Lyra, Melina, Naida)
  with their own quest chains.
- Nyx and Atenea as major goddesses on equal footing with Artemis and
  Hippolyta.
- Chapter 2 — the first journey out of Sardis.
- Equipment upgrade system, exploration missions, Abyss mode.
- Steam Deck + Linux builds.

## How to play

- **Touch or mouse**: tap cards to select, PLAY to score, DISCARD to cycle.
- **Combat tip**: higher poker hand = more damage. A royal flush wins almost
  any fight. Use DISCARD to hunt for the ranks your current hand is
  *close to*, not the ranks you already have.
- **Save anywhere**: the game auto-saves on every scene change. Close the
  tab, reopen tomorrow — everything is still there.

## Controls

| Action | Touch / Mouse |
|--------|---------------|
| Select card | Tap |
| Confirm hand | Tap PLAY |
| Discard and redraw | Tap DISCARD |
```

## Devlog post draft (publish alongside the build)

**Title**: Dark Olympus 0.0.1 — First Playable on itch.io

```markdown
## Dark Olympus 0.0.1 is live on itch.io.

After [N] months of design, prototype, and one full system rewrite, Chapter 1
is shippable. This is the first public build of Dark Olympus — a narrative
RPG where poker hands deal damage and the Greek gods don't remember their
own names.

### What's in 0.0.1

Chapter 1, end to end. Meet Artemis, fight the Gaia Spirit, watch the
pantheon start to stir. One full romance arc through stage 2. Four tavern
tournament combats as an optional side track. 30–45 minutes.

### What I cut to ship

- The gacha system. Originally companions leveled via a currency-pull loop;
  it added friction without adding meaning. Level + romance is the only
  progression now.
- Three axes of companion power became two. Simpler is better.
- Eleven of the fourteen designed companions are not in this build. They're
  coming — but they were blocking the ship.

### What's next (0.0.2 target)

- Chapter 2 + the road out of Sardis.
- The seven quest companions (Daphne, Circe, Thetis, Echo, Lyra, Melina,
  Naida) with their own questlines.
- Nyx and Atenea fully playable.
- Whatever I learn from your feedback on this demo.

If you play, tell me: what did you love? What confused you? What do you
want in 0.0.2?
```
